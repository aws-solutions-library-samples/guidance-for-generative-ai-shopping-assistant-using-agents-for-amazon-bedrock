Mediterranean Salad

Ingredients:
- 5 oz mixed greens (such as spinach, arugula, romaine)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, diced 
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup cooked chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Dressing:
- 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, chickpeas, feta, olives, parsley and basil. Toss to mix.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper.

3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.

4. Serve immediately.
