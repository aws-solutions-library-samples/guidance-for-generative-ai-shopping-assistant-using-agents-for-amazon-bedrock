AnyCompanyCommerce Policies and FAQs Shipping Policy:

    Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days for delivery within the continental United States. $5.99 flat rate.
    Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days for delivery within the continental United States. $12.99 flat rate.
    Free Standard Shipping on orders over $75.
    International Shipping: Rates and delivery times vary based on destination. Please contact customer support for more information.
    All orders are shipped via a trusted carrier, and tracking information will be provided once your order has been shipped.

Return Policy:

    You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.
    Exceptions: Electronics, software, and clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.
    Items must be in new, unworn, and unwashed condition with all original tags and packaging.
    Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery by contacting customer support or using the online return portal.
    Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or was shipped incorrectly.
    Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment within 7-10 business days after the returned item has been received and processed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs): 
Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order by visiting our website, clicking on the "Order Tracking" link, and entering your order number and email address. You can also contact customer support for assistance. 

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed?
A: Unfortunately, once an order has been placed, it cannot be canceled or modified. If you need to make changes, please contact customer support as soon as possible. 

Q: What is your exchange policy?
A: If you receive an item that is defective or not as described, you can exchange it for a new item within 30 days of delivery. Contact customer support to initiate an exchange. 

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping services?
A: Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee of $3.99 per item. You can select this option during the checkout process. 

Q: How do I redeem a promotional code or gift card?
A: You can enter your promotional code or gift card number during the checkout process. If you encounter any issues, please contact customer support for assistance. 

Q: What is your privacy policy?
A: Our privacy policy can be found on our website. We take the privacy and security of our customers' information very seriously and do not share or sell any personal data to third parties. 

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can contact our customer support team by email at support@anycompanycommerce.com or by phone at 1-800-555-1234. Our support hours are Monday-Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.
